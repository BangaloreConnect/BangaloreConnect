QS Engineer (Quantity Surveyor)

Bangalore Connect Verified Job

Job Role & Responsibilities

This is a full-time, on-site role for a QS Engineer responsible for managing costs and budgets for construction projects.

  • Handling Pre & Post contracts, preparing cost estimates and cost plans.
  • Performing cost control, budget management, and resource allocation tasks.
  • Involved in contract administration and procurement processes.
  • Liaising with contractors and stakeholders to meet project specifications/timelines.

Key Requirements

Location: Bengaluru/Goa/Hyderabad/Chennai

Experience: Experience in real estate/construction is a plus

Company: Prestige Estates Projects Limited

Education: Bachelor's degree in Electrical/Mechanical Engineering

Note: Diploma in EEE adds value.

Core Qualifications

  • Cost Estimation, Cost Control, and Contract Administration skills.
  • Strong analytical and numerical skills with attention to detail.
  • Proficiency in relevant QS software and tools.
