Job Role & Responsibilities

The Representative position handles customer service and technical support via inbound/outbound calls, emails, or chats, focusing on client products or services.

  • Clarify customer requirements, provide resolution, and maintain knowledge of client products/services.
  • Greet customers professionally, demonstrating empathy, and building rapport.
  • Maintain complete and accurate documentation in the call tracking database.
  • Participate in activities to improve customer satisfaction and business performance.
