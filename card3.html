Technical Support Specialist

Bangalore Connect Verified Job

Job Role & Responsibilities

As the first point of contact for users, you will provide empathetic, timely, and technically sound solutions for the HackerRank platform via email, chat, and phone across all user segments.

  • Engage with customers via phone, email, and chat, providing clear guidance using Pylon.
  • Serve as the first line of triage and response for the inbound ticket queue.
  • Perform in-depth troubleshooting and communicate technical findings clearly.
  • Stay updated on new releases and share insights to improve usability.

Key Requirements

Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)

Experience: 2+ years in Technical Support for Saas/Enterprise

Company: HackerRank

Skills: Proficiency in one programming language, basic SQL/APIs.

Work Model & Schedule

  • Willingness and flexibility to work nights, weekends, and holidays as part of a global coverage model.
  • Ability to manage high ticket volumes while maintaining quality and empathy.
  • Familiarity with ticketing tools such as Pylon or Linear (a plus).
