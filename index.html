ATS Friendly Resume

Freshers Resume

  • Use a clean and simple layout.
  • Add important keywords for your field.
  • Highlight your technical skills clearly.
  • Mention projects and internships first.
  • Keep the resume only one page.
  • Save the file as PDF or DOCX.
Get Fresher Template

Experienced Resume

  • Use STAR or PAR for bullet points.
  • Show achievements with numbers.
  • Keep the resume 1–2 pages long.
  • Highlight leadership and projects.
  • Tailor your resume to the job role.
  • Add key skills and tools you use.
Get Experienced Template

Executive Resume

  • Start with a strong profile summary.
  • Add KPIs and measurable results.
  • Mention team, budget, or P&L roles.
  • Highlight strategy and innovations.
  • Keep it within two pages.
  • Show major achievements clearly.
